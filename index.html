Who We Are

Loving God. Loving others.
Magnifying the name of Jesus.

The mission of Clarkrange Baptist Church is to love God, to love others, and to magnify the name of Jesus Christ. Our vision is to glorify our God and Savior, to make true disciples throughout all nations, to minister the ordinances, to edify believers, and to do all that is sovereignly possible and biblically permissible to magnify the name of Jesus.

“For God so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten Son, that whosoever believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life.”
John 3:16

We study the Word, practice what we learn, and in the process, grow together. Our church family has been associated with the Southern Baptist Convention since our founding, and our beliefs are reflected in the Baptist Faith & Message.
